Hextris


An addictive puzzle game inspired by Tetris. Play it at www.hextris.io, or https://hextris.github.io/hextris.

By:

  • Logan Engstrom (@lengstrom)
  • Garrett Finucane (@garrettdreyfus)
  • Noah Moroze (@nmoroze)
  • Michael Yang (@themichaelyang)
